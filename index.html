hush

a phone-first backup console for your homelab.

Every machine on your tailnet, on one legible map — coloured by backup posture, not CPU. Protected boxes sit quiet; the ones at risk, unprotected, or failed are the ones that stand out. It answers the question a homelab usually can't at a glance: is everything backed up, and could I get it back?

Runs hush-agent and/or hush-control as a systemd service. Must run as root.
